Commercial & Industrial Air Quality Testing

Comprehensive testing for offices, schools, factories, and commercial properties. AHERA compliant. SCDES & NCDHHS licensed.

100% Independent Testing — Zero Conflict of Interest. We never sell remediation or removal services. Our results and recommendations are always honest and unbiased.

Air Quality Testing Built for Business

Commercial and industrial properties face unique air quality challenges — from regulatory compliance requirements to employee health concerns to pre-construction testing needs. A1 Air Quality Consultants brings over 25 years of experience in commercial environmental testing to every project.

Commercial Services We Provide

  • AHERA project designs — compliant inspection plans and project designs for schools and public buildings
  • Air duct testing — NADCA test to verify cleanliness of air supply
  • Air quality testing — identify VOCs and other contaminants affecting employee health and comfort
  • Asbestos testing and air monitoring — PCM and TEM analysis for pre-renovation surveys and abatement oversight
  • Clearance testing — post-abatement verification that spaces meet safe re-occupancy standards
  • Lead testing — surface, dust, and paint analysis for older commercial buildings
  • Mold project protocols — detailed scope of work documents for remediation contractors
  • Mold testing — air sampling, surface testing, and moisture analysis for commercial spaces
  • Radon testing — measurement for offices, schools, and facilities

Industries We Serve

We work with property managers, school districts, healthcare facilities, manufacturing operations, government agencies, real estate professionals, and general contractors across North Carolina, South Carolina, and parts of Georgia. Our reports are designed to give decision-makers the accurate data they need to protect occupants and meet compliance requirements.

Why Commercial Clients Choose A1

As a testing-only company, we have zero conflict of interest. We never sell remediation services, which means our findings are never influenced by a financial motivation to find problems. Commercial clients trust us because our data is honest, our reports are thorough, and our 25+ years of experience means we know what to look for in complex commercial environments.

Turnaround Time

Most commercial test results are delivered within 48 to 72 hours. We understand that commercial projects are time-sensitive and work to deliver results as quickly as possible.
